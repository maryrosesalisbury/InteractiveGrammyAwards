Grammy Awards 2016

By: Mary Salisbury

March 8, 2016

The 58th Annual Grammy Awards were held on February 15, 2016 at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, California. Singers, actors, models, and other celebrities gathered from all over the world too see which talented artists would go home with a prestigious Grammy Award this year. According to an article on Billboard.com, taking the lead this year was Kendrick Lamar with 11 total nominations, as well as Taylor Swift and The Weeknd with seven nominations each.
